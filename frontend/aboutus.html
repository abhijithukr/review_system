About HostelHub

Real Reviews. Better Choices. Happier Stays.

Every year, hundreds of students arriving at the College of Engineering Trivandrum face the same challenging rite of passage: finding a safe, verified, and comfortable place to stay.

Without an organized resource, freshers and their parents are often forced to spend hot days walking the lanes of Chavadimukku, Sreekariyam, and Pangappara, relying solely on local brokers and word-of-mouth. Offline information is scattered, and online reviews are often sparse, outdated, or unreliable.

Recognizing this struggle, a group of senior CET students decided to build HostelHub. We realized that what students needed most was transparency—a single, organized platform where they could view detailed profiles, inspect rooms virtually, and compare parameters objectively.

HostelHub acts as a community-driven database built with absolute transparency in mind. By collecting detailed amenity lists, verifying curfew rules, calculating precise distances to the CET campus, and presenting real reviews from real seniors, we aim to eliminate housing uncertainty.

Our Mission

To make student housing transparent, safe, and accessible for everyone.

We believe finding a home near campus should be a seamless experience. Our goal is to ensure no student has to make a blind choice regarding their living conditions.

What We Built

Simple, powerful tools designed specifically to help CET students discover their ideal living space.

Browse Hostels

Explore a curated directory of verified accommodations near campus with detailed profiles, curfew details, and pricing lists.

Compare Hostels

Evaluate rent, deposit amounts, curfew times, and distance to college side-by-side to make the most informed decision.

Map Explorer

Visualize hostel locations around campus. Understand precise walking distances and proximity to local transit.
