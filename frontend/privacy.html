Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

How HostelHub collects, uses, and protects your information.

What this means for you

We believe privacy policies should be easy to read. HostelHub is built by students to help students, not to track them. We collect only what is necessary to connect you with hostel owners or publish reviews. We will never sell your personal details, and we keep your credentials secure.

1. Introduction

Welcome to HostelHub. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of students seeking accommodation near the College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, share, and protect your information when you use our platform.

By accessing or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any terms of this policy, please do not use the platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you interact with our services, submit enquiries, or post reviews. This may include:

  • Identity Information: Name, student status, and contact details (email address, telephone numbers).
  • Enquiry Details: Specific preferences, selected hostels, date of enquiry, and any custom messages sent to hostel owners.
  • User-Generated Content: Hostel reviews, ratings, suggestions, and feedback submitted on the platform.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser versions, screen resolution, operating system, and general usage patterns.

3. How We Use Information

We use your collected information to provide and improve the HostelHub platform, including:

  • Facilitating direct communication between students and verified hostel owners.
  • Publishing student reviews and ratings to help other users make informed decisions.
  • Analyzing platform usage trends to optimize layout structure and website performance.
  • Verifying user identity to maintain review authenticity and protect listings from spam or fraudulent feedback.

4. Reviews

Hostel reviews and ratings published on the platform are user-generated content. To ensure the integrity of the review database, we collect names and verification details before publishing.

While reviews are public to help CET freshers, we do not disclose contact numbers or sensitive private credentials associated with your reviewer profile to other public users.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketing agencies. Information is shared only under the following conditions:

  • Hostel Owners: When you submit a booking enquiry, your contact details and message are shared directly with the respective hostel owner to initiate the booking process.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or official request by CET administrative authorities to verify hostel records or maintain security.

6. Data Security

The security of your data is of paramount importance to us. We implement standard security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, please be aware that no transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies

We use basic cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your browser to remember your search preferences, comparison selections, and active sessions.

You can configure your web browser to reject cookies or warn you before accepting them, though some sections of the HostelHub website might not function optimally without cookies enabled.

8. Third Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us (e.g., Google Maps links or hostel owner websites). We advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every external site you visit.

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites or services.

9. Policy Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data stored on HostelHub, please reach out to us:

  • Email: privacy@hostelhub.in
